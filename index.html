About

Qunliang Xing obtained his BEng and Honors degrees from Beihang University, Beijing, China in 2019. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree at Beihang University, advised by Changjiang Distinguished Professor Mai Xu. His research interests include image/video enhancement and coding. He has published two papers in IEEE TPAMI and one in ECCV, and one of them is top 1% highly cited according to the Essential Science Indicators (ESI). His papers have received over 300 citations. He has been granted one national invention patent and two software copyrights. He has won two NTIRE championships. His open-source projects have received more than 600 stars. He was selected for the Tencent Rhino-bird Open-source Training Program. He is awarded the OpenMMLab Active Contributor. He serves as a reviewer for five IEEE transactions and conferences. In addition, he also excels in sports, having won second place in the Beihang 100m final and the Beihang soccer championship as the school team captain.
