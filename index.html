Qunliang Xing is currently a Ph.D. student at Beihang University, advised by Professor Mai Xu. He obtained his BEng and Honors degrees from Beihang University in 2019.

His research interests include Computer Vision and Multimedia. He has published several papers in top journals and conferences such as TPAMI and CVPR, including a top 1% highly cited paper recognized by the Essential Science Indicators (ESI). His papers have received over 580 citations. He has won two NTIRE championships. His open-source projects have received more than 780 stars. He was selected for the Tencent Rhino-bird Open-source Training Program. He is awarded the OpenMMLab Active Contributor. He serves as a reviewer for CVPR, TIP, TMM, and others.

He also excels in sports, having won second place in the Beihang 100m final and the Beihang soccer championship as the school team captain twice.
