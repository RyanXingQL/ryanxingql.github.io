Qunliang Xing

Image & Video processing artist.

Qunliang Xing received the BEng degree and Honors degree from Beihang University, Beijing, China in 2019. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree at Beihang University, advised by Professor Mai Xu. His research interests include image/video enhancement and coding. He has published several papers in IEEE TPAMI and ECCV, one of which is top 1% highly cited according to the essential science indicators (ESI). He has won two NTIRE championships. His open-source projects have received more than 600 stars. He is awarded the Active Contributor by OpenMMLab. He has won the second place in the Beihang 100m final and the Beihang soccer championship as the school team captain. He is a reviewer for several IEEE transactions including TIP, TMM, TCSVT and JAS.

本人于 2019 年获北京航空航天大学工学学士和荣誉学位；正于本校攻读博士学位，师从徐迈教授。主要研究图像、视频增强和编码。一作论文发表于 IEEE TPAMI 和 ECCV，其中 ESI 前 1% 高引 1 篇。取得两项 NTIRE 挑战赛冠军。开源项目获得 600 余次星标。获 OpenMMLab Active Contributor 荣誉。获北航百米决赛亚军。带领院队获得北航足球赛事总冠军。担任 IEEE TIP、TMM、TCSVT、JAS 等期刊审稿人。
